VIP Kiteboarding Package

Our VIP Kiteboarding Package includes ten hours of lessons plus unlimited supervision for a week, with this supervision system you will practice using our kite gear + assistant as much as you want without counting the hours, this is the most efficient way of learning kiteboarding.
