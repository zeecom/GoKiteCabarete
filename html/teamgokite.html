Gokite’s Team know all about Kiteboarding’s teaching, they're commited with quality in our services, your security and having fun!

Ramon L. Diaz (aka Richard)

Born and raised in the Dominican Republic, he started kiteboarding in 2001. He became an IKO instructor in September 2004 and worked for 8 years as manager at a local kite school on Kite Beach. In 2009 Richard founded GoKite Cabarete Kiteboarding School, he is fluent in Spanish and English even to know the most effective techniques about kite teaching methods which has adapted and improved in order to create a learning appropiate model for all types of students.

Yaneris L. Pacheco L.

Born and raised in Montecristi, Dominican Republic. Yaneris is who makes possible that our grafic image and social media works in an integrated way and she is in charged to administrate our website. Since 2012 she is the administrative manager of Gokite Cabarete.

Raul Urbano (aka Quichipa)

Born and raised in the Dominican Republic started kiteboarding in 2003. Raul became an instructor in 2006 after competing in the PKRA. When not teaching kiteboarding, Raul can be seen entertaining the crowds with his ‘Big Air’ tricks. Raul speaks Spanish & English.

Kelvin Corniel Fermin

Born and raised in the Dominican Republic started kiteboarding in 1999, he is an high performance athlete and Kiteboarding Instructor. He works for Gokite since 2009 as Kiteboarding Instructor , teaching in Spanish, English and Norwegian.

Francisco Nolasco (aka Papo)

Born and raised in the Dominican Republic started kiteboarding in 2000. He worked as assistant at a local kite school and joined GoKite Cabarete in May 2010. Papo is a repair technician and can be found assisting on the beach or taking care of kite and board repairs.

Jose Luis Castillo (aka Fresa)

Born and raised in the Dominican Republic. He started kiteboarding and joined GoKite Cabarete in 2013. He has became in one of the best Kite instructor of Kite Beach. Jose Luis speaks Spanish and English.
