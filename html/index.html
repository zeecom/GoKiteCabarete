About Us

Gokite Cabarete Kiteboarding School was born in 2008 in Cabarete Dominican Republic, at the hand of its founder and CEO Richard Diaz who has over 11 years of experience teaching Kiteboarding. We have the best location of all Kite Beach Cabarete.

We also have amazing facilities like a large green area to set, showing and to maintain to our Kiteboarding equipments even a large area in front of the beach that is perfect to train with our students, a reception plus shop and a beach bar restaurant next to us .

Do not forget that Cabarete is loved for windsurfer, surfer as well as kiteboarder, with constant trade winds and warm waters. Gokite Cabarete is the ideal place to learn Kiteboarding because our mission is offer you Kitesurfing lessons safe and fun in a friendly atmosphere where you can get the best kitesurfing holidays of your life.

We are located at Extreme Hotel on Kite Beach Cabarete.
